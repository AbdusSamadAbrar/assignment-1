Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in microfinance, eco-friendly jute products, and startups.

Sustainability

Bangladesh promotes sustainability through renewable energy, eco-friendly jute, and reforestation.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh develops infrastructure with bridges, highways, ports, and renewable energy.

Empowerment

Bangladesh empowers through microfinance, education, gender equality, and technology.

The Vision of Future

The vision for Bangladesh's future emphasizes sustainable development, technological innovation, and inclusive growth, aiming to empower youth, strengthen infrastructure, combat climate change, and create a prosperous, equitable society for all its citizens.

Digital Transformation

Bangladesh embraces digital transformation through e-governance, IT sector growth, smart education, and nationwide internet connectivity.

Green Energy

Bangladesh advances green energy with solar power, wind farms, biogas, and eco-friendly energy solutions.

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh builds modern infrastructure with bridges, highways, ports, renewable energy, and smart urban planning initiatives.
